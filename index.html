About Me

Hi, I’m Chen Chaya Cohen. I hold a B.A. with high honors in Linguistics & Comparative Literature from Bar-Ilan University. For 20 years I have built my career in the insurance sector—primarily in Life and Long-Term Care claims—and for the last four years, I’ve been working as a Medical Underwriter. This work requires precise review of complex medical and policy documents, sound judgment, and a holistic view of the entire process and the policyholder’s needs.

Over the last year, I started collaborating with our Requirements and QA teams to improve work processes within my professional department. I’ve always loved optimizing procedures and paying attention to the small details in my work, and I quickly realized that the QA field perfectly aligns with this. I am now taking a QA course with the goal of making a professional career shift and becoming a QA professional.
